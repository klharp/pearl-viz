VISUALIZING SUPPORTING DATA FOR PEARL RESILIENCE SCORE

This dashboard is intended for an overview of U.S. climate-related disasters and their impacts.

The visualizations are intended to help Pearl develop the Pearl Resilience Score (PRS) to help to identify a home's resilience against climate-related hazards.

These hazards have been identified as:

  • Extreme heat / drought
  • Extreme cold / winter storms
  • Storms (wind, hail, lightning)
  • Floods (inland, coastal)
  • Tornados
  • Hurricanes / Tropical storms
  • Wildfires
  • Earthquakes

[taken from the scope document]
Uses of the PRS will include the following:

  • A homeowner or homebuyer will be able to use the PRS to make decisions about purchase or renovation.
  • An insurance company will be able to use the PRS to make underwriting decisions.
  • A public planning agency will be able to use the PRS to allocate rebates and incentives for improvements, and may also be able to use the PRS to inform land use and other public policy decisions.
